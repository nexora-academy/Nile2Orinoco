Our Vision 2026

Starting the Bridge from Ras Sidr

Nile2Orinoco is a newly launched initiative based in Ras Sidr, Egypt. We are starting our journey in 2026 to connect the Nile and the Orinoco, building the first cultural tourism bridge of its kind.

Our mission begins with research and planning to provide a unique and safe experience for future travelers.
