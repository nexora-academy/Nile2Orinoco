Cayo de Agua: The Sandbridge Paradise

Experience the breathtaking "Isthmus," a natural sand bridge that divides the Caribbean Sea into two turquoise horizons. Cayo de Agua is consistently ranked among the most beautiful beaches in the world.

Beyond its iconic white sandbar, dive into crystalline waters perfect for snorkeling or find solitude in a sanctuary of pure tranquility. It is the crown jewel of the Los Roques National Park, where the sky meets the ocean in a seamless blend of blue.
